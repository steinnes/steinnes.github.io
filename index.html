From Monolith to Services @ QuizUp

Tuesday, February 10, 2015
Last week I did a talk at a local IT conference, UT Messan. The title of my talk was the rather inflated “From Monolith to Services at Scale: How QuizUp is making the (inevitable?) transition, one endpoint at a time”. In the talk I try to tell the story of how we at QuizUp are transitioning to a more service-oriented architecture, and which steps we decided to take first. The route we chose was to setup ZooKeeper as the heart of our system for service registration, discovery and configuration, and then figure out how to route client requests to services running inside Docker containers.
